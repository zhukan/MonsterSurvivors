How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting Started: Use your mouse or touch controls to move your character around the battlefield. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies, so focus on positioning and avoiding damage.

Survival Strategy: Stay mobile to avoid being overwhelmed by monster hordes. Collect gems and experience orbs dropped by defeated enemies to level up and gain new abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely based on your playstyle and the current situation.

Power-ups and Upgrades: Each level-up presents you with three random upgrade options. These can include new weapons, stat boosts, or special abilities. Experiment with different combinations to find the most effective build for your survival strategy.

Advanced Tips: Learn enemy movement patterns to predict their behavior. Some monsters are faster but weaker, while others are slow but deal massive damage. Adapt your strategy accordingly and always keep an escape route in mind!
